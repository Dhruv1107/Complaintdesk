MVSR Complaint Desk is an online web portal where a student can raise different types of queries related to college premises.Here a student can report any type of problem online, which will be reported to concerned department or authorities.

'Register a Complaint' tab is available in the navigation menu to raise different type of queries.Any student can raise any number of queries by clicking on the 'Register a Complaint' option. Students can view their raised complaints by clicking on 'View Complaints' tab available in the menu.

List of Incharges of concerned departments can be viewed by the students by clicking on the 'Incharges' tab in the menu bar.

'FAQs' option is made available in the navigation menu about the query raising procedure.Students can go through it to have a basic knowledge about how the query is being processed.

Students can also change their passwords by clicking on the settings menu where they may find 'Change password' option.

Students can edit their general details by clicking on the 'Edit Profile' option available in the settings menu.
