1.Will be I sent a notification about query closure?

Answer-You won't be sent any notification about query closure. You will just find the status of the query changed from 'Raised' to 'Resolved'.

2.Can I/Should I close the query?

Answer-Only the respective Incharge can close the query.Students can't close the query.

3.How to change the password?

Answer-You can find the change password option in the settings menu.You will be guided after clicking that option.

4.How to change/edit my e-mail Id?

Answer-You can change/edit your e-mail by clicking on Edit Profile option present in settings menu.

5.How to know the details of respective Incharges?

Answer-You can go through different concerned Incharges list by clicking on 'Incharges' tab on the navbar.

6.Will I have to report to the respective Incharge after query raising?

Answer-You need not report the Incharge for any general query.But if it's a query which invloves a third person then you may be called by the Incharge for further process.

7.What's the use of my phone number while registering?

Answer-You should give your phone number as the Incharge may have to personally meet you for some type of queries.

8.Is it mandatory give my phone number?

Answer-Yes it is madatory to give your phone number while registering.

9.What can I do to recover my password?

Answer-You can click on 'Forgot Password' link in the login page available at the bottom through which you will be guided the further process.

10.Can I view queries raised by different students on the same issue?

Answer-No, you will be only able to view your queries.
